Privacy Policy

Device Tracker  ·  Last updated: May 27, 2026

Summary: Device Tracker does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Bluetooth scanning data stays on your device. The only external service we use is Apple's App Store for in-app purchases, and Adapty for subscription management.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Device Tracker ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to your privacy and handle all data in accordance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

By using the App you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Bluetooth & Proximity Data

The App uses your device's Bluetooth hardware to discover nearby Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices and estimate their proximity. During a scan the App reads:

  • Advertised names of nearby BLE devices
  • Received Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI) values used to estimate distance
  • Device identifiers assigned by iOS (these rotate periodically and are not linked to any real-world identity)

This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. It is held in memory only for the duration of a scanning session and persisted locally in iOS UserDefaults solely to power the "My Devices" saved-device list.

2.2 Preferences & Settings

The App stores the following preferences locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Saved device list and per-device notification preferences (notify when found / notify when lost)
  • Distance unit preference (meters or feet)
  • Language selection
  • First-launch state

None of these preference values leave your device.

2.3 Purchase & Subscription Data

The App offers auto-renewable subscriptions. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.).

We use Adapty to manage subscription status. Adapty may receive limited technical data necessary to verify your subscription — typically an anonymised device identifier and purchase receipt metadata. Adapty does not receive your name, email address, or payment information. You can review Adapty's privacy practices at adapty.io/privacy.

2.4 Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account creation or collect any login credentials.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact details.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect location data (GPS, Wi-Fi positioning, or cell-tower data).
  • We do not collect crash logs or analytics beyond what Apple provides to all developers through Xcode Organizer (which is aggregated and anonymised by Apple).

4. Local Notifications

If you enable "Notify when Found" or "Notify when Lost" for a saved device, the App schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device by iOS — no data is sent to a server to trigger them. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → Device Tracker → Notifications.

5. Background Bluetooth Access

The App requests the bluetooth-central background mode so that proximity monitoring can continue when the App is in the background. This access is used solely to detect and notify you about your saved devices; no data collected in the background is transmitted off the device.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored by the App lives in iOS UserDefaults on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App. Uninstalling removes all locally stored preferences, saved devices, and cached subscription state.

Adapty may retain anonymised subscription receipt data for a limited period in accordance with their own retention policy. Contact Adapty at support@adapty.io for data deletion requests related to their service.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you, or to restrict or object to certain processing. Because we store data only on your own device (with the exception of anonymised subscription metadata held by Adapty), most rights can be exercised simply by uninstalling the App or adjusting iOS settings. For any remaining requests, contact us at the address in Section 11.

California residents (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising.

EEA / UK residents (GDPR): Our lawful basis for processing subscription status is the performance of a contract (to deliver the service you purchased). All other processing is strictly necessary for the app to function and takes place on-device only.

10. Security

Because personal data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers, security of that data is governed by iOS's own security model (data protection, sandbox isolation, and secure enclave). We do not operate any backend infrastructure that stores identifiable user data.
